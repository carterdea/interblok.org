The Plan

The shipping container is stronger, safer, and faster to the field than traditional building materials. Taking modern fabrication methods to shipping containers brings us access to steel infrastructure in every port in the world.

As we work with local grassroot NGOs we bring design and supplies that are influenced by their needs. We source labor and materials locally to stimulate the economy and create jobs. interBLOK is busy down in Mexico working to provide dependable off-grid water and power solutions for our partners.
